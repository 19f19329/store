WHO WE ARE ?
Alyousfi Perfumes Store

We master the language of perfumes and create scents that embrace your memory and shake hands with your heart

I am a young Omani man who loves the world of perfumes, which is a deep world that carries many feelings. Many family members and friends used to consult me ​​about buying perfumes because they saw that I have a high and luxurious taste in perfumes. I started this project in 2018 by selling perfumes from home to family, neighbors and some friends, and then I went to the general public by selling on Instagram.
